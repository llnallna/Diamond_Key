How To Get Turkish Citizenship?

Foreigners can obtain Turkish citizenship after the amendment to the naturalization laws in Turkey in 2018 through several methods, including property investment, bank deposit, and investment in economic projects

Real Estate Investment

Buying a property or properties which
value exceeds 400 thousand dollars.

Investing in Turkey

By investing in a project with a capital
of at least 500 thousand US dollars.

Bank Deposit

By depositing at least 500 thousand US dollars in a Turkish bank for 3 years.
